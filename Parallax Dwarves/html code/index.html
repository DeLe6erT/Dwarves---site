The foot-tall gnomes are small humanoids with brownish skin, round noses, bony legs, sharp teeth and, perhaps most strikingly, a large potato-like growth on their heads. Gnomes are hairless and rarely wear any clothes in the summer, although most graphic images politely omit the most inappropriate parts of their anatomy, which, in truth, are visible.
